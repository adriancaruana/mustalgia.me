mustalgia.me

Nostalgia, n:

A sentimental longing or wistful affection for a period in the past.

Relive your past music obsessions

Last.fm is great for recording the music you listen to and showcasing the music you're currently enjoying. Unfortunately, Last.fm does not make it easy to explore your historical listening patterns. This makes it difficult to recall and revisit the music you enjoyed in the past. At worst, this could mean that you forget about music that hasn't found its way into your long-term memory.

That is where mustalgia.me comes in! Much like the photo app on your smartphone reminds you of past memories, mustalgia.me analyses your listening history to trigger your nostalgia. This is done by porviding various insights into your past and present listening habits, and hopefully reminding you of some artists and albums that you may want consider revisiting.
